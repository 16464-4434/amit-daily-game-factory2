How to Play

You are a Chroma Courier, tasked with delivering vital data packages across the neon-lit cyber-grid. Your mission: collect packages and drop them off at matching colored zones, while avoiding dangerous obstacles!

  • Movement: Use WASD or Arrow Keys on keyboard, or 👆 drag your ship with touch/mouse.
  • Collect: Fly over a colored package to pick it up. You can only carry one at a time.
  • Deliver: Fly over a drop-off zone that matches the color of your carried package to deliver it and score points.
  • Avoid: Collide with obstacles and you'll lose health!
  • Pause: Press P or Escape, or click the button.
