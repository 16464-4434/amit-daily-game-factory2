AstroBloom Bot

Guide your tiny repair bot through a procedurally shifting space garden, collecting resources, crafting tools, and completing quests to restore its vibrant ecosystem.

Tutorial: Cosmic Gardening Basics

Welcome, little bot! Your mission is to restore the AstroBloom garden.

Movement: Use WASD or Arrow keys (keyboard) / Directional pad (touch) to move.

Interaction: Press 'E' or the Action button (touch) to gather resources, repair plants, or activate objects.

Resources: Collect Pollen, Metal Scraps, and Crystal Shards from nodes. They regenerate!

Crafting: Open the Menu ('M' or Menu button) to craft repair parts and new tools from your inventory.

Quests: Track your objectives in the Menu. Completing them earns Bloom Energy.

Upgrades: Spend Bloom Energy in the Upgrades menu to boost your bot's speed, battery, cargo, and unlock new abilities like a grapple hook!

Sector Shifting: The garden sectors will periodically drift and re-orient, changing the map! Adapt and find new paths.

Bloom Level: Restore damaged elements to increase the garden's overall Bloom Level. Higher levels unlock new sectors!
